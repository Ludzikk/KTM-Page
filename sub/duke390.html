ktm 125 duke 2024

ktm 125 duke 2024

The 2024 version of the KTM 125 DUKE is even more thrilling, all thanks to the latest generation of the 125 cm3 four-stroke engine! Greater power, more dynamic acceleration and lighter construction compared to previous generations are its characteristic features. The unit was created based on the brand's extensive experience in the field of small-capacity engines. The gearbox and cylinder head have also been improved. In addition, the KTM 125 DUKE engine is fully adapted to the latest EURO5.2 emission standards. But that's not all - the KTM 125 DUKE is not only ready to ride, but above all READY TO RACE! Discover the difference today and head out to the city behind the wheel of this feisty naked 125 segment!

freedom

comfort

position

On the 2024 KTM 125 DUKE, every detail matters! Thanks to the increased wheelbase and innovative frame geometry, cornering has reached a new level of stability and precision. Continuing the legendary tradition of DESCENT OF THE BEAST, with an aggressive riding position, the 2024 KTM 125 DUKE pushes the boundaries for NAKED BIKE enthusiasts. Convenience? You can rest assured! Thicker seat foam, narrower profile and improved ergonomics ensure comfort for both the driver and passenger. Moreover, in parallel to the design work on the 125 DUKE 2024 model, work was also carried out on the development of a wide range of KTM PowerParts. As a result, the offer includes tuning seats with various height ranges. This way, you can adjust this equipment perfectly to your dimensions.

safety

brakes

The KTM 125 DUKE 2024 features a brake disc with an impressive diameter of 320 mm on the front wheel, supported by new four-piston radial calipers. This new disc features improved wear resistance, longer pad life and excellent thermal management. At the rear, a 240 mm disc works in harmony with the innovative two-piston caliper, offering similar advantages to its front counterpart. Moreover, the KTM 125 DUKE 2024 is equipped with advanced Cornering ABS and SUPERMOTO ABS systems, ensuring not only braking efficiency, but also maximum safety in corners. This is an evolution in braking technology that exceeds previous standards.

durability

wishbone

The modern curved cast aluminum swingarm holds the essence of durability and solidity for the rear suspension. The direct shock mount, strategically moved to the side, not only guarantees a stylish look, but also optimizes space, creating room for a new silencer. This innovative muffler is a unique accent, giving the machine a unique character and refined elegance. Discover perfection combined with functionality in its purest form!
