KTM 300 EXC SIX DAYS ARGENTINA 2024

KTM 300 EXC SIX DAYS ARGENTINA 2024

With a twenty-year history dotted with triumphs and wins, the KTM 300 EXC SIX DAYS has firmly established itself on the podium of achievements. Backed by powerful drivetrain for dynamic response at low revs and astonishing speed at high revs, this bike is simply READY TO RACE. Thanks to these features, it is the choice of champions aspiring to win the highest laurels. For 2024, the KTM 300 EXC SIX DAYS has been equipped with new tools to beat its rivals, including a new orange frame, dual-chamber suspension, new bodywork, TBI technology, and an impressive SIX DAY kit, allowing it to compete with anyone, on any terrain, with incredible confidence in your own abilities.

power

engine

The engine with a power of 49 HP, a torque of 43.8 Nm and a weight of 24.6 kg appeal to the imagination. The new 2-stroke 300 cm³ engine not only expands the possibilities in the E3 enduro class, but is a perfect solution for drivers looking for maximum power and torque with the lowest possible weight of the motorcycle. Hence, the KTM 300 EXC engine is the undisputed leader in the ultra-competitive E3 and Hard Enduro enduro classes around the world.

power

engine

The heart of this motorcycle is a surprisingly light and compact engine weighing only 28.8 kg. This technical masterpiece, designed with increased efficiency in mind, has been redirected slightly rearwards in the 2024 version and the location of the output sprocket has been redesigned to lower the center of gravity. The combination of the high-revving nature of the 51-horsepower engine and a sophisticated design that minimizes internal friction guarantees incredibly smooth and lively responses to every touch of the throttle.

comfort

suspension

The 2024 model has a real suspension revolution. PDS suspension, renowned for its unrivaled racing performance, now features an all-new shock with two-stage manual compression adjustment. Combined with the new WP XACT double-chamber fork with 48 mm diameter drops, it provides stable and effective damping in all conditions.
