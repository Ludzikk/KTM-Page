ktm 350 exc-f 2024

ktm 350 exc-f 2024

Meet the 2024 KTM 350 EXC-F - a motorcycle that combines refined power, precise control and reliable durability. Designed for true enthusiasts who are looking for adrenaline experiences on the trail. This uncompromising enduro equipment is equipped with a powerful yet smooth engine, guaranteeing unrivaled performance both on paved roads and on the most demanding off-road trails. A highly advanced suspension system and precise brakes provide unparalleled control, allowing you to overcome challenging terrain with incredible ease.

freedom

stability

frame

The 2024 model has a completely redesigned, black powder-coated frame. The frame was activated to obtain electrical loads, which translates into feedback for absorption devices and delivery at speed devices. These features are achieved through the use of rotating masses inside the frames and an advanced forged connection to the frame head.

power

engine

The heart of this motorcycle is a surprisingly light and compact engine weighing only 28.8 kg. This technical masterpiece, designed with increased efficiency in mind, has been redirected slightly rearwards in the 2024 version and the location of the output sprocket has been redesigned to lower the center of gravity. The combination of the high-revving nature of the 51-horsepower engine and a sophisticated design that minimizes internal friction guarantees incredibly smooth and lively responses to every touch of the throttle.

comfort

suspension

The 2024 model has a real suspension revolution. PDS suspension, renowned for its unrivaled racing performance, now features an all-new shock with two-stage manual compression adjustment. Combined with the new WP XACT double-chamber fork with 48 mm diameter drops, it provides stable and effective damping in all conditions.
